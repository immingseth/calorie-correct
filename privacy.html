Privacy policy

How Calorie Correct handles your data. Plain language. No legalese where we can avoid it.

Last updated: May 6, 2026

The short version: we keep what we need to make the app work, we don't sell it, we don't share it with advertisers, and we never train AI models on your conversations. You can export or delete everything any time.

What we collect

Stuff you log

Weights, meals, exercises, water, notes, and chat messages with Coach. This is the data the app exists to track. It's stored on your device by default. If you sign in, a copy is also stored on our servers so you can use Calorie Correct on multiple devices.

Account information

If you create an account, we store your email address and a display name. We use Google OAuth or email magic links — we never see or store passwords.

Profile and goals

Height, age, sex, activity level, target weight, and target rate of loss. These are used to calculate your calorie targets and personalize Coach. Nothing else.

Technical basics

To run a website we have access to standard server logs (IP address, browser type, request timestamps). We don't run third-party analytics that profile you, no Google Analytics, no Facebook pixel, no advertising trackers.

What we don't collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendar, or other phone data
  • Photos other than ones you explicitly attach to a meal
  • Anything from third-party services unless you connect them yourself

How we use it

Your data is used to make Calorie Correct work for you specifically. That means:

  • Showing you your own logs, charts, and trends
  • Calculating your TDEE, calorie target, and calibration accuracy
  • Sending your meals and questions to Anthropic's Claude API so Coach can respond (see below)
  • Backing up your data across devices if you sign in
  • Sending you transactional email (sign-in links, account confirmations)

Coach and the Claude API

Coach is powered by Anthropic's Claude. When you send a message to Coach, that message — along with relevant context like your recent meals and goals — is sent to Anthropic's API. Anthropic processes the request and returns a response.

Per Anthropic's commercial terms, Anthropic does not train its models on data sent through the API. We don't pass your conversations to any other party.

What we don't do

  • No ads. Calorie Correct will never run advertising. The product is free or subscription-funded — that's it.
  • No selling data. We don't sell your information. To anyone. Ever.
  • No training on your conversations. Your chats with Coach are not used to train AI models.
  • No tracking pixels. The site does not embed third-party tracking from advertisers or social networks.

Cookies and storage

The app uses your browser's local storage to keep your data on-device. If you sign in, we set a secure session cookie so you stay logged in. We don't use cookies for cross-site tracking.

Your rights

You can:

  • Export all of your data at any time from Settings → Export
  • Delete your account, which permanently removes your data from our servers
  • Use the app without an account — everything works locally if you'd rather not sign in

Children

Calorie Correct is not directed to children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, please email us and we'll remove the account.

Changes

If we change this policy meaningfully, we'll update the "last updated" date and notify signed-in users by email.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests: hi@caloriecorrect.com
